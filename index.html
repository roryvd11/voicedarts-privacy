Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Voice Darts is built on a simple principle: your data stays on your device.

What we collect

Voice Darts does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There is no backend. There is no analytics. There is no account required to play.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Microphone audio — used in real time to recognise your spoken score. Audio is processed by Apple's on-device speech recognition (iOS 17+). It is never recorded or saved.
  • Speech recognition — processed by Apple's Speech framework. On-device recognition is used by default. If on-device assets are unavailable, iOS may send audio to Apple's servers for processing under Apple's own privacy policy (apple.com/privacy).
  • Player names and game settings — stored in your device's local storage (UserDefaults). Never transmitted.
  • Lifetime stats (180s, averages, win counts) — stored locally on your device. Never transmitted.
  • Sign in with Apple (optional) — if you choose to sign in, Apple provides a user identifier and optional name/email. This information is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server operated by Voice Darts.

Third parties

Voice Darts does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children

Voice Darts does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included with the next app update.
